Welcome to the Michigan Minute

We realize that in the busy lifestyles that we lead as professionals and students, we seldom find time to fully read a news article. Thus, we have created a system that can summarize any Michigan Daily article for you to read within a minute, helping you stay up-to-date with the University.

To summarize an article, simply copy its url from the Michigan Daily's website , paste in the textbox below and hit summarize!
